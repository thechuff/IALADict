# How to move Hence: up a line in Notepad++
1. Ctrl+H
2. Tick 'regular expression'
3. Find what: `\RHence:`
4. Replace with: ` *Hence:*`

# How to replace parts of speech with italics in Notepad++
1. Ctrl+H
2. Find what: ` v `, ` n `, ` adj `, ` adv `, ` see `
3. Replace with: ` *v* `, ` *n* ` , ` *adj* ` , ` *adv* `, ` *see* `
